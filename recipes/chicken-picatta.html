Chicken Picatta Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (sliced horizontally to make 4 cutlets)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (for dredging)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter (for searing)
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine or chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2 lemons)
  • 1/4 cup brined capers, drained
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter (for sauce finish)
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  • Lemon slices, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Chicken:
    • Season chicken cutlets with salt and pepper.
    • Dredge lightly in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Sear Chicken:
    • Heat olive oil and 2 tbsp butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Sauté chicken 3–4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
    • Transfer to a plate and tent with foil.
  3. Make the Sauce:
    • In the same skillet, add white wine (or chicken broth) and lemon juice.
    • Scrape browned bits from the bottom and bring to a boil.
    • Add capers and simmer for 3–5 minutes until slightly reduced.
  4. Finish the Sauce:
    • Turn off the heat and stir in remaining 2 tbsp butter until smooth.
  5. Serve:
    • Return chicken to the skillet and spoon sauce over the top.
    • Garnish with parsley and lemon slices if desired.
    • Serve hot, ideally with angel hair pasta, rice, or sautéed vegetables.
